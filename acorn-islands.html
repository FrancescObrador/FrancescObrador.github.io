Follow Mr. Mofletes on it's homecoming. Explore Acorn Island along with this cute flying squirrel to collect all the golden acorns it needs to spend the winter with enough food in a warm burrow with its mother and sister. Run, jump, glide, climb and talk in this adventure along an island full of adorable animals. Help them and they will reward you!

We formed a multidisciplinary team where I worked closely with artists (3D and 2D artists, a musician and a writer). My role was to plan, manage and develop the project as a Vertical Slice with my university mates.

Roles: Game Designer, Level Designer, Unity Developer & Producer
Genre: Casual, 3D Platformer
Development time: 6 months
Team Size: 3
Tools Used: Unity, Yarn Spinner, Fmod, Google Sheets, Git Kraken


Responsabilities

  • One of the members defining the core game mechanics
  • Planning and estimating tasks for Sprints
  • Scripting elements behaviours of the game
  • Design, write and implement the characters' dialogues
